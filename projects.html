IUT Model OIC Club is a Platform for academic simulation of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC), which aims at providing students with an environment where they can build, practice and unleash their potentials with diplomatic and negotiation skills, public speaking and leadership skills through the simulation of the OIC Conferences.

Mock Sessions

Mock Sessions are weekly debates where the Club Members seize the opportunity for experiencing practical training in representation of specific OIC Member State (allocated by the Presidency) and debate on the topic set for that certain simulation

Diplomatic Training

Diplomatic Training is the process of teaching the rules and procedures of the simulation format. It is the most fundamental and necessary part of the academic agenda of the club. 2.1. Diplomatic Training must be given to each and every Member. If a Member is absent to the Training Sessions due to unavoidable reasons, then he/she must watch the video material, while the President should ensure that the absentee is full aware of the rules of procedure.

Academic Training

Academic Training is the process of carrying out seminars regarding the agenda items and topics of the OIC and the ICYF-DC itself. The Club accepts responsibility of fully providing a detailed lesson regarding the expected MOIC topics, so as to maximize the efficiency and quality of the simulation sessions

Activities

We have as activities The diplomatic training, Academic Training and Mock Sessions
