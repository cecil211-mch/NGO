Establishment and Inauguration of the club(2017)

IUT MOIC clubâ€™s establishment was a dream come true. The club was officially inaugurated in IUT with the presence of the National Coordinators then; Miss. Fahmida Faiza and SIM Sheikh Shadman. Whereby light was thrown on what Model OIC is about. And to implement a few diplomatic skills a game was played whereby two groups of people had to vote for their leaders. Here is where the journey commence. IUT Model OIC Club is a Platform for academic simulation of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC), which aims at providing students with an environment where they can build, practice and unleash their potentials with diplomatic and negotiation skills, public speaking and leadership skills through the simulation of the OIC Conferences.
